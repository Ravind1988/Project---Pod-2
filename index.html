About US

A 5 star boutique Resort set on a paradise of 28.8 acre with award winning landscaped gardens offering Malaysian hospitality. The Resort features spacious accommodation with teak furniture, hardwood floor and private balconies overlooking gardens, pool or lake view. All rooms are equipped with complimentary wireless internet service, in room safe and air-conditioning.
